Help & User Guide

📌 Navigation

Use the navigation bar at the top of every page to move between sections:

  • Home – Learn about Sa'ad Language Center and our mission.
  • Gallery – View language class photos and course schedule table.
  • Booking – Fill out the form to enroll in a course.
  • Help – This page, with instructions and tips.
  • About – Meet the founder, Sa'ad Said Araye.

📝 How to Use the Booking Form

  • Full Name & Email: Enter your real details so we can contact you.
  • Language: Choose the language you want to study.
  • Gender: Select your gender (required).
  • Hobbies: Optional – check any that apply.
  • Comments: Add any special needs or questions.
  • All fields marked with * are required.

🖼️ Gallery & Table Tips

  • Hover over images to see a zoom effect.
  • The table shows course levels, durations, and fees.
  • Click external links for more language learning resources.
