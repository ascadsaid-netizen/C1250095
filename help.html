❓ Help & User Guide

🧭 Navigation Guide

Use the navigation bar at the top to move between pages:

  • Home – Overview of the Language Learning Center.
  • Gallery – View images and data tables about our courses.
  • Booking – Fill out the form to register for a course.
  • Help – You are here! Read instructions on how to use the site.
  • About – Learn about the creator of this website.

📋 How to Use the Booking Form

  1. Fill in your Full Name (required).
  2. Select your Major from the dropdown list.
  3. Choose your Gender by selecting one of the radio buttons.
  4. Check any Hobbies that apply to you.
  5. Write any Additional Comments in the text area.
  6. Click "Submit Booking" to send your request.
  7. Click "Reset" to clear all fields and start over.

🖼️ Gallery & Data Table Instructions

  • The Gallery displays images of our learning environment. Hover over them to see a zoom effect.
  • The Data Table shows course schedules, fees, and links to the booking page.
  • Click "Book Now" inside the table to quickly go to the booking form.
  • External links to Duolingo and Babbel are provided for extra learning resources.
